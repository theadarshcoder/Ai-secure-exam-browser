# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Exam conductors, university administrators, mentors (proctors), and the students/candidates attending the exams.

## Product Purpose
An AI-secure exam browser and administration portal that universities and institutions purchase to conduct secure, monitored, and tamper-proof online examinations. Success means flawless session stability, intuitive controls for proctors, and a seamless testing environment for candidates.

## Brand Personality
Professional, intuitive, highly secure. It should feel authoritative but not intimidating, projecting reliability and trust.

## Anti-references
Should NOT look like legacy academic LMS software (e.g., outdated Blackboard or Moodle). Should NOT be a playful consumer app. Avoid cluttered UI with excessive borders or "glassmorphism as default."

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over cleverness:** In high-stress exam environments, every control must be obvious.
- **Authoritative feedback:** Security states (like verification and monitoring) must feel absolute and indisputable.
- **Progressive disclosure:** Only show complex telemetry and logs when the proctor explicitly needs them.
- **Performance implies security:** A snappy, zero-lag interface builds trust in the system's underlying capabilities.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Must maintain high contrast for legibility under varying screen conditions during exams. Critical actions must be keyboard accessible and error messages must be explicit (not relying solely on color like red/green).
